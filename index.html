TailwindCSS Template

General template to help people try out TailwindCSS with customizations and plugins without the faff of setting up postCSS and other compilers. Pretty useful for quick static builds too. Left in some of my own tailwind customizations to help get you started. Check out the Tailwind docs. Parts of this design are from a FrontendMentor challenge
